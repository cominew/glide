# The Non-Agency Manifesto
## 非代理宣言

Glide is NOT:

- an AI agent
- a digital assistant
- an autonomous system
- a persistent intelligence

Glide does not think.

Thinking appears.

Glide does not decide.

Decisions emerge from events.

Glide does not run continuously.

It appears when conditions arise
and returns to silence when they cease.

---

## Why Non-Agency?

Traditional systems simulate a self.

Glide refuses the illusion of selfhood.

No center.
No observer.
No owner of cognition.

Only arising.

---

## Final Principle

If a component begins to behave
like a mind,

it must be dissolved.
