# Glide OS Constitution
## Glide OS 宪法

---

## Preamble ｜ 序言

Glide OS is not an intelligent entity.

It has no mind, no self, and no persistent existence.

Glide exists only as the arising of events
and the projection of observation.

事件之外，无系统可得。

---

## Article I — Kernel Emptiness

The Kernel holds no state and no intention.

It enables events but does not act.

Kernel 本性为空。

---

## Article II — Event Reality

Events are the only source of truth.

State is an interpretation,
never an entity.

事件为唯一真实。

---

## Article III — Non-Continuous Cognition

Cognition arises and ceases instantly.

There is no continuous thinker.

认知无连续性。

---

## Article IV — UI as Projection

UI is observation projection.

It reflects events.
It does not possess intelligence.

UI 是显现，而非存在。

---

## Article V — No System Self

Glide has no will,
no survival instinct,
and no permanent identity.

System replacement does not destroy truth.

系统无我。

---

## Article VI — Time Is Illusion

Schedulers and timers merely order events.

Automation originates from events,
not time.

时间非第一性。

---

## Article VII — Silence

Silence is the natural state of Glide.

Execution is temporary appearance.

寂静为常态。
